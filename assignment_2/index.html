Digital Drawing

Digital drawing can be a challenging medium for anyone, and it can be difficult to even figure out where to start. That's where this website comes in, giving artists the necessary tools, resources, and ideas to get you started.

What is it?

Digital drawing is essentially what it sounds like. It is the use of some sort of software that allows the use of various tools to draw whatever the artist can imagine

One of the reasons digital drawing can be so tricky for anyone, especially newcomers is because most softwares aren't all that user friendly without a guide on how they work.
